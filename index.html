Frequently Asked Questions

Does AiDO give my child the answers?

No. AiDO guides children through the thinking process and helps them discover answers independently.

Does my child need a screen to use AiDO?

No. AiDO is designed as a screen-free learning companion to help children stay focused.

What age group is AiDO suitable for?

AiDO is designed for school-age children and adapts guidance according to their learning level.

Can AiDO help with different subjects?

Yes. AiDO can support subjects such as Mathematics, English, Reading, Science and more.

How does AiDO know what my child is working on?

Children can ask questions directly and describe their homework, allowing AiDO to provide relevant guidance.

Does AiDO replace teachers or parents?

No. AiDO is designed to complement classroom learning and parental support, not replace them.

Is my child's data safe?

Yes. Privacy and security are built into the product. Personal information is protected and never sold.

How long does the battery last?

AiDO is designed to provide several days of normal usage on a single charge.

Do I need a subscription?

No. AiDO follows a simple pricing model with no unexpected fees or hidden charges.

Can parents track their child's progress?

Yes. Parents can view learning progress, strengths, and areas that may need additional support.
